ğŸ¯ The 70-20-10 Rule

  • 70%: Proven content (these ideas, formats that work)
  • 20%: Experimental content (new formats, risky ideas)
  • 10%: Trend-jacking (viral challenges, breaking news)

Upload Consistency Matters More Than Quantity

Don't burn out trying to upload daily. Pick a sustainable schedule:

  • Once a week: High-quality, well-researched content
  • Twice a week: Mix of tutorials and lighter content
  • Three times a week: Only if you have systems in place

Batch Create Content

Film 4-6 videos in one day, then spread them out. This:

  • Maintains consistency without daily stress
  • Allows you to ride creative momentum
  • Gives you breathing room for strategy

ğŸ” SEO & Discoverability Tips

1. Title Optimization

  • Put keywords at the beginning
  • Keep under 60 characters (doesn't get cut off)
  • Use numbers and power words (Free, Easy, Simple, Ultimate)
  • Add year if relevant ("2025")

2. Thumbnail Strategy

  • Contrasting colors (stand out in feed)
  • Minimal text (3-5 words max)
  • Expressive faces (emotions drive clicks)
  • Consistent branding (colors, fonts)

3. Description Optimization

  • First 2-3 lines are visible (hook here)
  • Include target keyword naturally
  • Add timestamps (increases watch time)
  • Link to related videos (session time)

4. Use Playlists

Group similar videos into playlists:

  • "Complete Beginner's Guide to [Topic]"
  • "[Niche] Tutorials"
  • "Weekly [Series Name]"

Playlists increase session watch time = algorithm boost
