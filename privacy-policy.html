1. Introduction

Welcome to Namerly ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website namerly.app and use our name generation and content creation services.

By using Namerly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Generated Content: The names, usernames, pickup lines, bios, and other content you generate using our tools
  • Language Preferences: Your selected language (English or Spanish)
  • Feedback: Any feedback, comments, or suggestions you provide

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website (pages visited, features used, time spent)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, device type
  • IP Address: Your internet protocol address for analytics and security purposes
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device for functionality and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our name generation services
  • Personalization: To remember your language preferences and recently generated content
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve our services
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Your preferences and recent content are stored locally on your device
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated usage data is retained for up to 26 months
  • No Account Data: We do not store personal accounts or user profiles
  • Automatic Deletion: Generated content is automatically cleared from local storage periodically

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:
